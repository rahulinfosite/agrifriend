About AgriFriend

Get Informed Decisions About Your Farming Strategy


AgriFriend system will be able to recommend the crop based on the parameter using our machine learning model which will be trained using these parameters data. The system will also provide the farmers to connect with government employed agriculture committee if they have any query. As an extension we would also like to add the level of fertilizers and pesticides based on nutrient value of the soil in that area. This system will create win situation for both government as well as farmers as the crop productivity will increase and which will solve the problem of food which will arise due to growing population of our country. Also this system can provide the benchmarks to provide and formal training campaign organized by government the farmers may be notified through such application even it will facilitate the training through videos uploaded for training farmers.
